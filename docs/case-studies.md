# Prigenix Case Studies & Results

These are representative examples of the outcomes Prigenix delivers. Client names are anonymized where requested.

## Manufacturing: Predictive maintenance reduces downtime by 34%

**Client:** A mid-sized precision manufacturer with 120+ CNC machines.

**Challenge:** Unplanned machine downtime was costing the company over $50,000 per month in lost production and emergency repairs.

**Solution:** Prigenix deployed vibration and temperature sensors on critical machines, connected them to an edge gateway, and built a predictive maintenance model that flags anomalies 24–48 hours before failure. The system integrates with the client’s maintenance management system and sends alerts to technicians via Slack.

**Results:**
- 34% reduction in unplanned downtime in the first six months.
- $300,000+ in avoided emergency repair costs.
- Maintenance scheduling shifted from reactive to condition-based.

## Logistics: Invoice automation saves 600 hours per month

**Client:** A third-party logistics provider processing 15,000+ invoices monthly.

**Challenge:** A team of eight people manually entered invoice data into the ERP, causing delays, errors, and disputes.

**Solution:** Prigenix built an AI document extraction pipeline that reads PDF and scanned invoices, validates them against purchase orders, and posts approved records to the ERP. Exceptions are routed to a review queue.

**Results:**
- 600+ hours of manual data entry saved per month.
- 94% straight-through processing rate.
- Invoice processing time reduced from 3 days to under 4 hours.

## SaaS: AI-powered support triage cuts response time by 50%

**Client:** A B2B SaaS company with 5,000+ customers.

**Challenge:** Support tickets were piling up, and urgent issues were sometimes buried in the queue.

**Solution:** Prigenix integrated an AI classifier into the support inbox that reads incoming emails, assigns priority and category, drafts a first response, and routes complex issues to the right specialist team.

**Results:**
- 50% reduction in median first-response time.
- 30% of tickets resolved without human intervention.
- Support team satisfaction improved because agents spent more time on meaningful problems.
