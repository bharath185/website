# Prigenix Delivery Process

Our process is designed to reduce risk, deliver early value, and keep clients informed at every step. It is a continuous loop, not a one-way handoff.

## 1. Discover

We start by understanding the business outcome you need. This usually takes a few days and includes:

- Stakeholder interviews with operations, engineering, and business leads
- Workflow mapping and bottleneck identification
- System and integration inventory
- Data and compliance review
- ROI modeling and prioritization

**Deliverable:** A short discovery report with recommended quick wins, a medium-term roadmap, and a proposal for the first sprint or pilot.

## 2. Design

Once the scope is clear, we design the architecture and user experience.

- Solution architecture diagrams
- Data models and integration contracts
- UI/UX wireframes and prototypes for user-facing systems
- Security and compliance plan
- Sprint plan with milestones

**Deliverable:** A design document and a sprint plan approved by the client.

## 3. Build

We work in two-week sprints with live demos, tests, and documentation from day one.

- Daily async updates via Slack or Teams
- Weekly sprint reviews
- Continuous integration and automated testing
- Incremental deployments to staging

**Deliverable:** Working software at the end of each sprint, with automated tests and deployment pipelines.

## 4. Deploy

We deploy to production with monitoring, rollback plans, and runbooks.

- Production deployment with zero-downtime strategies when possible
- Monitoring, alerting, and observability setup
- Documentation and handoff materials
- Training for client teams

**Deliverable:** A production system that is monitored, documented, and supported.

## 5. Optimize

After go-live, we measure, tune, and expand.

- Performance and cost optimization
- User feedback loops
- New feature development
- Additional workflow automation
- Scaling to more users, sites, or data volume

**Deliverable:** Continuous improvement and a long-term technology partnership.

## Communication rhythm

- **Daily:** Async standups in shared channel.
- **Weekly:** Sprint review and planning call.
- **Monthly:** Business outcome review (metrics, ROI, roadmap).
- **On-demand:** Slack/Teams access to the Prigenix team.
