# Prigenix — Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Prigenix do?

Prigenix is an AI automation and software engineering studio. We help businesses eliminate repetitive work, connect disconnected systems, and build custom software and AI products that scale. Our core services are custom software engineering, AI agents, workflow automation, industrial IoT, data engineering, and platform modernization.

## Who do you work with?

We work with startups that need a senior engineering partner and enterprises that need to modernize operations without disrupting business. Our clients are typically in manufacturing, logistics, SaaS, finance, retail, healthcare, and professional services.

## How is Prigenix different from a typical software agency?

We combine AI, automation, IoT, and software engineering into one delivery team. We do not just write code; we design the full solution, deploy it to production, operate it, and continuously optimize it. We also speak the language of operations and manufacturing, which means we can work directly with plant managers, logistics teams, and support leaders — not just IT.

## How long does a typical project take?

- Pilot workflow or prototype: 2–4 weeks
- Production MVP or internal tool: 6–12 weeks
- Enterprise program or platform modernization: 3–6 months for the first release

Timelines depend on scope, data readiness, and integration complexity.

## How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope. We offer:
- Pilot projects from $15,000
- Monthly retainers for ongoing work
- Enterprise programs with dedicated pods

We provide fixed-price proposals for well-defined pilots and time-and-materials or retainer pricing for ongoing work.

## Do you work remotely?

Yes. We are a remote-first studio with team members across multiple time zones. We operate fully online and use Slack, Teams, Zoom, and Notion to stay aligned with clients.

## What happens after the project is live?

Every engagement includes monitoring, documentation, and a warranty period. Retainer clients get ongoing support, optimization, and new feature development. We do not disappear after launch.

## Do you support legacy systems?

Yes. We regularly integrate with ERPs, CRMs, SCADA systems, PLCs, and custom legacy applications. Platform modernization is one of our core service lines.

## Is my data secure with Prigenix?

We follow security best practices including encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access control, and secure CI/CD pipelines. We can align with SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, and other compliance frameworks as needed.
