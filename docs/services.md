# Prigenix Services

Prigenix offers six core service lines. Each can be engaged separately or combined into a single program depending on the client’s needs.

## 1. Custom Software Engineering

We design and build software that fits your business like a bespoke machine — not an off-the-shelf compromise. Every architecture decision is made around performance, security, maintainability, and how your teams actually work.

**What we build:**
- Customer and partner portals
- SaaS products and multi-tenant platforms
- Internal tools and operations dashboards
- Mobile apps for iOS, Android, and field teams
- APIs and backend services

**Typical stack:** React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, Python, Go, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes.

**Typical timeline:** 6–12 weeks for a production pilot.

## 2. AI Agents & Autonomous Systems

We build agents that go beyond chatbots — systems that perceive context, plan actions, and complete multi-step tasks across tools and teams. Whether it is automating support triage, research, code review, or operations, the agent operates within guardrails you control.

**Common use cases:**
- Document extraction and classification
- Customer support triage and response drafting
- Smart routing of requests and alerts
- Generative features inside existing products
- Knowledge bases with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)

**Typical stack:** OpenAI, Anthropic, vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector), LangChain, custom orchestration.

**Typical timeline:** 4–10 weeks depending on data readiness.

## 3. Workflow Automation

We map your manual handoffs and repetitive tasks, then replace them with reliable, auditable automations. Integrations span APIs, databases, legacy systems, and messaging platforms so nothing falls between the cracks.

**Common workflows:**
- Invoice capture → validation → ERP sync
- Email and form intake → classification → routing
- Customer onboarding → CRM updates → notifications
- Report generation and distribution
- Approval flows and exception handling

**Typical stack:** Node.js, Python, n8n, custom schedulers, webhooks, REST/GraphQL APIs.

**Typical timeline:** 2–6 weeks per workflow.

## 4. Industrial IoT & Edge Systems

We connect sensors, controllers, and edge devices to give you live visibility and control over physical operations. From predictive maintenance to environmental monitoring, our systems deliver low-latency data where it is needed.

**Common solutions:**
- PLC and SCADA integration
- Sensor telemetry pipelines
- Predictive maintenance models
- OEE and production dashboards
- Edge gateways for offline-first sites

**Typical stack:** MQTT, OPC-UA, Modbus, AWS IoT, Azure IoT, InfluxDB, TimescaleDB, Grafana.

**Typical timeline:** 6–14 weeks depending on hardware and site complexity.

## 5. Data Engineering & Intelligence

We clean, structure, and pipeline your data so it becomes a strategic asset instead of a liability. Our work supports analytics dashboards, reporting, machine learning, and AI-powered products.

**Common deliverables:**
- Data warehouses and lakes
- ETL/ELT pipelines
- Real-time streaming pipelines
- Analytics dashboards and self-service reporting
- Data quality monitoring

**Typical stack:** dbt, Airflow, Spark, Snowflake, BigQuery, PostgreSQL, Kafka, Metabase, Tableau, Looker.

**Typical timeline:** 4–10 weeks.

## 6. Platform Modernization

We untangle legacy code, infrastructure, and dependencies and migrate them to modern, cloud-native platforms. The process is incremental and risk-managed, so your business keeps running while your technology evolves.

**Common outcomes:**
- Monolith decomposition
- Cloud migration (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Containerization and Kubernetes adoption
- CI/CD pipeline modernization
- Database migrations and performance tuning

**Typical timeline:** 8–16 weeks for the first incremental release.
