# Prigenix — Company Overview

## Who we are

Prigenix is an AI automation and software engineering studio built for companies that want to move faster without adding headcount. We are a remote-first team of product engineers, AI specialists, cloud architects, and industrial IoT engineers who act as an embedded engineering partner for startups and enterprises.

We do not sell slide decks or hand off thick requirement documents. We design, build, deploy, and operate the systems that run your business. Our clients treat us as an extension of their own engineering and operations teams.

## What we believe

- **Software should earn trust.** Every system we ship is tested, monitored, documented, and built to be maintained by your team or ours.
- **Automation is not a product category; it is an outcome.** We start with the business result — fewer errors, faster turnaround, lower cost — and work backwards to the right mix of AI, integrations, and custom code.
- **Industrial operations deserve modern tooling.** Factories, warehouses, and field assets generate enormous value but are often underserved by consumer-grade software. We build rugged, secure systems for physical operations.
- **AI is most useful when it is invisible.** The best AI deployments feel like reliable coworkers: they handle the repetitive work and escalate the exceptions to humans.

## How we work with clients

We offer three engagement models so clients can match risk, speed, and budget to their stage:

1. **Pilot (2–4 weeks)** — A focused workflow, integration, or prototype delivered to production. This is the fastest way to prove ROI.
2. **Retainer** — Ongoing engineering capacity for product development, automation expansion, and continuous improvement.
3. **Enterprise program** — A dedicated pod for large-scale transformation, platform modernization, or multi-site industrial deployments.

Every engagement starts with a discovery call or automation audit. We map your workflows, identify the highest-ROI opportunities, and propose a clear plan with timelines, deliverables, and pricing.
